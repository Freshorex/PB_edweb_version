Progress report!

Good morning, evening or night!

I see you've grown curious about what we've been working on, I don't see why I couldn't share just a tiny bit with you, so let's get started!

As of writing this post, we've successfully captured our vision for our protagonist: Thomas Rockets!


Nothing much has changed, but we now have an official color palette and reference for all the cutscenes in the game, as well as his modal!
You might've seen how complicated he seems to draw, atleast in game-logic, so we'll be animating him like Scribblenauts!
In Scribblenauts, the characters are cut into pieces to be able to animate directly on the engine, so we'll be doing that to make it easier!

As far as development goes, you'll know on our next devlog! We'll be expanding more on the gameplay of the game as well as ideas and concepts YOU'll be able to participate on!

Until next time!

Welcome to our site!

Good morning, evening or night!

It's so good that we can now meet for the first time! We're the team responsible for this wonderful new site and the contents of it that will be popping up as we work on the game!

As you can see, you're on our blogs page! We've decided not to use a blogging system, so these news are delivered from us to you by hand! Just like a little birdy showing off our message!

First things first, for those who are new to our project, you may be confused on what all of this is... well, dear Reader, you're in luck! You're on the blog page that we'll be using to update you on our new game project: Projec Birthplace! But, oh! What is this game you are talking about, dear Writer?

Project Birthplace is our opening game as a gamedev studio, a side-scroller 2D-shooter where you have to survive against powerful and scary creatures in an abandoned facility while trying to get it back in working condition. It is greatly inspired by Resident Evil, Guàishòu 怪兽 and OSC media.

We hope that this interests you enough to continue with us and watch as our creation flourishes and releases once it's once!
We have also put time and effort into making comics with our characters, so, if comics or silly content (with some angst on the side) is something you're interested in, we'll be happy to see you there!

Until next time!
