Thomas Rockets

27 years old, he/they

Thomas is an engineer trainee from an internship program under the company responsible for the Birthplace and its maintenance.
He's the protagonist of the game and is tasked with bringing the facility's electricity and functionality back.

Thomas may seem nervous and insecure, but he's extremely smart and agile. He can get used to any environment And knows how to read blueprints and vent maps easier than anyone else.
He also knows his ways around technology if need be and has experience in the software field as well as hardware.

Illusione Cerulean Illumina

An experiment from the Birthplace.
Illusione is a charming, confident and adventurous type. They're the type of person to jump into danger head-first if they're guaranteed to be alive afterwards, no matter the injury.
They're also as agile as Thomas but more-so under-water than on land. Something they have in-common with Thomas outside of their agility, is their capacity to adapt to any environment.

Samuel Mallow More

42 years old, he/him

The department head of the shipping and importing department.
He's a patient and kind-hearted person with a soul of steel. Nothing can get under his skin that isn't new to him.
He's responsible for making sure any shipped containers get out of the facility safely and soundly, as well as any imported goods for research and facility mantainance.
He knows how to transport dangerous materials without issue.

Vivian Holloway Stein

35 years old, she/her

The archivist and research lead of the Birthplace.
She's an extremely illusive and mysterious individual. She's known for appearing in odd places and documenting everything around the offices and research departments. If you're ever in doubt, she'll always have the information you need no matter what it is.
She's extremely closed off from everyone else for her own reasons, but she's one of the smartest in her department and really resourceful.
